🌍 Welcome to the WBO Tech Suite

Your Global Platform for Working by Outcomes at Roche Pharma International

Whether you're brand new to monday.com or a returning user, you're in the right place. This page is your single reference point for everything you need to navigate, contribute, and get the most out of the WBO Tech Suite.

What is the WBO Tech Suite?

Working By Outcomes (WBO) is Roche Pharma International's continuous planning and delivery method. It helps teams prioritize, learn, and collectively strive for maximum patient impact. An Outcome represents the impact, value, or benefit that our work ultimately creates — not the activity itself.

The WBO Tech Suite is the technology platform that makes this framework live and actionable. It replaces a fragmented ecosystem of Google Slides, Sheets, Trello, and Tableau with a single "one source of truth" on monday.com.

💡 The Tech Suite is not just another tool — it's your engine for growth and global collaboration.

How the Workspace is Organized

🏢 Regional Boards (50+ Affiliates)

Each country has its own Work Package Overview board where local teams manage their active, ongoing, and completed WPs:

  • EMEA: Germany, Greece, Poland, UK, Spain, Italy, France, Netherlands
  • APAC: Australia, Singapore, South Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong
  • LATAM: Argentina, Colombia, CCAV
  • Africa: Ghana, Nigeria, South Africa
  • Global clusters: COMET, iNET

🏆 Outcomes

  • Short Term Outcomes (STO) — Immediate deliverables within a cycle
  • Mid Term Outcomes (MTO) — Medium-horizon strategic objectives
  • Long Term Outcomes (LTO) — Long-range strategic goals
  • Cycle Learning [Global] — Retrospectives and captured learnings

🗄️ Repository & Workflows

Reference boards that power automations and data consistency:

  • Countries + Codes
  • Disease Area
  • Product
  • Function
  • Area
  • Workflow boards for automated routing, subitem creation, and outcome management
